Admissions & Requirements

Thank you for considering the University of Ghana Computer Science Department for your studies. Below are the admission requirements for our programs:

  • Applicants must have completed high school or its equivalent.
  • A minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale is required.
  • Submission of a completed application form.
  • Transcripts from all previously attended institutions.
  • Letter of recommendation from a teacher or academic advisor.
  • Personal statement outlining your interest in computer science.
